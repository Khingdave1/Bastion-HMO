Our Health Plans

Individual & Family

We have budget-friendly plans to support you and your family’s unique care needs. Our plans are comprehensive, covering primary health care, consultations with specialists, accident and emergency care, physiotherapy care, mental health, telemedicine and lifestyle management.

Corporate

We partner with organisations to create health insurance packages that work best for the organisation and its employees. We offer a wide range of benefits plans, nationwide networks, health data insights, innovative technology and a dedicated team that ensure all our benefits work together for better health, reduced costs, and improved processes.

Small Business

Whether you are just starting out or have been in business for years, we offer small businesses flexible and customisable health plans, financial wellness resources to keep your business and your employees healthy while managing health care costs. Our SME plans are designed for small businesses composed of 2 or up to 50 employees.
